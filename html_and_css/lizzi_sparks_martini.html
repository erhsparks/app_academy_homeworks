Ingredients

  • One martini glass, or a tumbler if you're accident-prone
  • Vodka or Gin, whatever your pleasure
  • Vermouth, a splash at most
  • If vodka: (at least) three olives and a liberal pour of olive juice
  • If gin: twist of lemon

Martini

Description

Classic martini. Stirred, not shaken, because the grad student in me doesn't want to have to wash more dishes than necessary. I'm pretty sure James Bond has a guy for that.

Directions

  1. If going the vodka and olives route: put olives in glass and pour in about enough olive juice to cover them. Else skip.
  2. Fill glass about 3/4 with vodka/gin. This is pretty much your only ingredient and there is no point in measuring it.
  3. Pour in a splash of vermouth. A splash! Stop! You've already added too much! That's why we only went 3/4 full in step 2 -- to correct the inevitable vermouth error.
  4. Correct vermouth error to taste with more vodka/gin.
  5. Add your twist of lemon if you're into that kind of thing. Else more olive juice, you can never have enough.
  6. Na zdorovie!
